Intermediate 5 Days / Week Natural Lifter Push · Pull · Legs

Push — Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Heavy compound first. Controlled reps on isolation work.

01

Barbell Bench Press

Full ROM · 2 min rest

4 × 6–8
02

Incline Dumbbell Press

15–30° incline · 90s rest

3 × 8–10
03

Overhead Press (DB or Bar)

Seated or standing · 90s rest

3 × 8–10
04

Cable Lateral Raises

Slow negative · 60s rest

3 × 12–15
05

Tricep Rope Pushdown

Squeeze at bottom · 60s rest

3 × 12–15
06

Overhead Tricep Extension

Cable or dumbbell · 60s rest

2 × 12–15
Tip

Warm up with 2 light sets before your working sets on bench. Never skip the warm-up.

Pull — Back, Biceps

Drive elbows back. Feel the lats stretch on every rep.

01

Weighted Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown

Full hang at top · 2 min rest

4 × 6–8
02

Barbell or Dumbbell Row

Chest supported or bent over · 90s rest

4 × 8–10
03

Cable Seated Row

Neutral grip · 90s rest

3 × 10–12
04

Face Pulls

Rope, pull to forehead · 60s rest

3 × 15
05

Barbell Curl

No swinging · 60s rest

3 × 10–12
06

Incline Dumbbell Curl

Full stretch at bottom · 60s rest

2 × 12
Tip

Think "elbows, not hands" when pulling. This forces the lats to do the work instead of biceps dominating.

Legs — Quad Focus

Squat deep. Don't skip hamstrings — they protect your knees.

01

Barbell Squat

Below parallel · 2–3 min rest

4 × 5–6
02

Leg Press

High foot placement · 90s rest

3 × 10–12
03

Bulgarian Split Squat

Dumbbell, slow descent · 90s rest

3 × 8 each
04

Leg Extension

Pause at top · 60s rest

3 × 12–15
05

Seated Leg Curl

Full ROM · 60s rest

3 × 12–15
06

Standing Calf Raise

Slow and full range · 60s rest

4 × 15–20
Tip

Pause at the bottom of your squat for 1 second. It kills momentum and forces true strength.

Push — Shoulder Focus

Prioritise delts today. Keep chest work moderate volume.

01

Seated DB Shoulder Press

Heavy · 2 min rest

4 × 6–8
02

DB Lateral Raises

3 sets + 1 drop set · 60s rest

4 × 12–15
03

Cable Front Raise

One arm at a time · 60s rest

3 × 12
04

Incline DB Fly

Stretch at bottom · 60s rest

3 × 12
05

Dips (Tricep focus)

Upright torso · 90s rest

3 × 10–12
06

Skull Crushers

EZ bar · 60s rest

3 × 12
Tip

On lateral raises, lead with your pinky finger slightly higher than thumb. Hits the medial delt harder.

Pull + Hamstrings

Hip hinge heavy. Keep your back straight. Finish with hamstrings.

01

Romanian Deadlift

Hip hinge, feel the stretch · 2–3 min rest

4 × 6–8
02

Single Arm DB Row

Knee on bench · 90s rest

4 × 10 each
03

Straight Arm Lat Pulldown

Cable, feel the lats · 60s rest

3 × 12–15
04

Lying Leg Curl

Full ROM · 60s rest

4 × 10–12
05

Hammer Curl

Alternating · 60s rest

3 × 12
06

Reverse Curl

Brachialis builder · 60s rest

2 × 12
Tip

On RDL, push your hips back not your knees down. The bar stays close to your legs the whole way.

Active Recovery

Full Rest Day

Sleep, eat, and recover.

Progressive overload rule: Add 2.5kg when you hit the top of the rep range for 2 sessions in a row. Track every session.
